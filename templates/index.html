Road Accident Detection and Safety Operations Management System

Helps individuals get required Medical Aid and others Support in Time.

Road accidents rates are very high nowadays. Timely medical aid can help in saving lives. This system aims to alert the nearby medical center about the accident to provide immediate medical aid and the Police Stations to buckle up the process of formalities that has to be followed before treating the victim.

Faster

Time for saving ones life will be reduced to half of the time by providing immediate medical aid with time, location, and district of the Accident
